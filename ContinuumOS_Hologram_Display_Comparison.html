🏆 ContinuumOS Recommendation HYPERVSN SmartV

Why HYPERVSN wins for ContinuumOS:

  • ✅ Most cost-effective for our use cases
  • ✅ Scalable from single units to wall-size displays
  • ✅ Works in ANY lighting (critical for funerals/events)
  • ✅ Proven technology with good support
  • ✅ Can create life-size ancestors with multiple units
  • ✅ Easy content management and remote updates

Secondary option: Proto Epic for premium/VIP experiences in Phase 4+

⚠️ Why We DON'T Need Giant Displays

  • Not entertainment focused - We're creating intimate, personal experiences
  • Authenticity matters - Life-size is perfect; larger feels impersonal
  • Cost efficiency - 4 HYPERVSN units (life-size) = ~$20K vs Proto at $65K
  • Entertainment companies can license - If Disney wants stadium holograms, they can use our API

"We're in the memory business, not the spectacle business."

📝 Special Features We Might Want to Integrate

HYPERVSN Cloud CMS

Remote content management system - perfect for updating ancestor responses, adding new memories, or seasonal messages without physical access to displays.

Proto's "Holopresence" Network

While expensive, Proto has existing installations in hotels/airports. Could partner for "Ancestor Kiosks" in high-traffic locations.

TeleHuman 2's Gesture Recognition

Their 360° sensor array for detecting viewer position could enhance our interaction model - worth licensing if it becomes commercial.

Glimm's Lightfield Capture

Their photogrammetry pipeline could improve our avatar creation from old 2D photos - potential acquisition or partnership.

Multi-unit Synchronization

HYPERVSN's ability to sync multiple units opens possibilities for "ancestor walking" between displays or appearing in multiple rooms.

Safety Features

HYPERVSN's protective casing and Proto's enclosed design are important for public spaces. TeleHuman 2's open design would need safety barriers.

Funding Options for ContinuumOS / LegacySphere

Strategy Cards (quick scan)

1. Grant‑first + Angel Follow‑up

Money source: SBIR / NEH grants (non‑dilutive) + heritage‑focused angels via SAFE.

  • Locks IP priority early, minimal equity loss.
  • Grant cycles are slow; angels write small cheques.

2. Mission‑Aligned Family Offices

Money source: 1–3 high‑net‑worth family offices passionate about legacy & preservation.

  • Large single cheques, low interference.
  • Need personal network; diligence can be long.

3. Foundation / Impact PRI

Money source: Gates‑Ed, Annenberg et al. recoverable grants or PRI loans.

  • No board control; repayment only if revenue hits.
  • Strict impact reporting; limited hardware CAPEX.

4. Silent‑LP SPV

Money source: Pool of tech adjacent HNWIs in a single SPV, capped info rights.

  • Fast close (\$7‑10 M); keeps cap table clean.
  • Higher legal cost; LP relations still on you.

5. Traditional VC (Dual‑Class)

Money source: Generalist VC, Series A \$12 M; founder retains 10 × voting shares.

  • Large scaling capital; top‑tier network.
  • Dilution & board seat inevitable.

6. Internal R&D Budget

Money source: Your current employer funds \$9 M seed + \$2 M kiosk pool from R&D.

  • Zero equity loss; company owns patents.
  • Potential bureaucracy; IP tied to employer.

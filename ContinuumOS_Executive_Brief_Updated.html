ContinuumOS / LegacySphere Platform Patent Pending

Mission

ContinuumOS (code‑name LegacySphere) is a modular XR platform that turns any collection of photos, text, audio and video into living, interactive memories. Users can converse with ancestors, founders or historical figures across VR headsets, hologram kiosks, web & mobile—while the system expands forever as new family or corporate data arrives.

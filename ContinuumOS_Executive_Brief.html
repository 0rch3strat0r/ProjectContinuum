ContinuumOS / LegacySphere Platform Patent

Mission

ContinuumOS (code‑name LegacySphere) is a modular XR platform that turns any collection of photos, text, audio and video into living, interactive memories. Users can talk to ancestors, founders, historical figures or brand storytellers across VR headsets, hologram kiosks, web & mobile—while the system grows forever as new family or corporate data arrives.
